B.Govindrajan (CEO of Royal Enfield)



B. Govindarajan is the Chief Executive Officer at Royal Enfield. He also serves as Wholetime Director on the Board of Eicher Motors Ltd. Govindarajan has spent over 23 years at Eicher Motors Ltd. and at Royal Enfield in two stints. In his present stint, he joined Royal Enfield in June 2011 as the Senior Vice President - Industry and has been the Chief Operating Officer at Royal Enfield since 2013.

in June 1989 and then joined Brakes India Ltd in June 1992. He joined Royal Enfield as Manager - Engineering in 1995 and was responsible for the Power train unit Manufacturing System Engineering. In 2004, Govindarajan became the General Manager - Manufacturing at Royal Enfield where he was responsible for the entire manufacturing activity and the implementation of the Lean and Quality Management Systems. In 2006, he was promoted as Divisional General Manager at Royal Enfield, post that, he moved to the Commercial Vehicle Manufacturing unit of Eicher Motors in Pithampur as the Head of Operations. .

During his tenure as the Senior Vice President - Industry, he was actively involved in ramping up production at Royal Enfield's Thiruvottiyur facility and raised the capacity to 1 lakh units per annum, a new production landmark at the company which paved way for the growth of the company. Under his leadership Royal Enfield has established the Technology centres at UK and in India and he led the team for successful delivery of the 650cc, twin cylinder Interceptor 650 and the Continental GT 650 which have won recognition across the globe ..
