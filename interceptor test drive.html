Interceptor

The Interceptor is a British motorcycle made by Royal Enfield between 1960 and 1970.In 1962, the company introduced the 750 Interceptor which evolved constantly until the end of production in 1970. In 2018, the Interceptor model was reintroduced as a 648 cc (39.5 cu in) parallel twin.
