Royal Enfield’s Build.Train.Race. Road Racing returns for 2023 with more riders and more races. The Royal Enfield Build.Train.Race. program is the first all-woman initiative teaching women to build & race their own motorcycle. The program highlights women and motorcycle culture in North America through a unique format that brings together riders from different backgrounds to compete in several different track events.
